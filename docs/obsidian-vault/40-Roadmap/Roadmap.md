# Roadmap

## Now

- ship the universal `Share something about AI` composer
- support multimodal intake for text, URLs, files, and mixed submissions
- open or continue an AI conversation after every submission
- store private source records with provenance and moderation outcomes
- derive the home feed from graph state instead of raw posts
- migrate existing tickets and entities into the new graph as seed evidence

## Next

- improve corroboration, contradiction, freshness, and trust weighting
- generate better guides, question pages, and answer artifacts from graph evidence
- emit hot-subject clusters and rising-topic cards
- make citation and evidence UI legible on public surfaces
- add moderation and review tools for graph-derived publishing

## Later

- subscription and personalization around entities, topics, and clusters
- richer public trend pages and recurring-subject dashboards
- external connectors and background ingestion sources
- curated research outputs and dataset exports
- stronger editorial workflows over graph-derived public artifacts

## Product Sequence

The product should mature in this order:

1. universal intake
2. grounded conversation
3. source-annotated graph accumulation
4. derived public publishing
5. advanced clustering, trust, and research outputs

## Things To Avoid Prematurely

- heavy front-door forms or rigid submission taxonomy
- public raw-post feeds that bypass synthesis and moderation
- flattening contradictory evidence too early
- over-designed editorial workflow before the graph has real volume
- product surfaces that compete with the universal composer
